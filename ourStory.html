Situated on the outskirts of downtown New Canaan, Cherry Street East Restaurant in Fairfield County has been dubbed the “hometown pub” of this charming little town. Since its establishment in 1977 Cherry Street East has been serving its family fare with rave reviews of best burgers in Fairfield County. You can enjoy sitting in a cozy booth, a quaint table surrounded by warm accents in a relaxed atmosphere, or sit at the cherry wood bar to watch your favorite teams on one of the HDTVs.

This family friendly establishment has something to offer every palate. Known for their Best Burger in Fairfield County we serve daily-ground burgers grilled to perfection, homemade French Onion Soup, the Patio Salad, fresh Fish and Chips or Panko-Encrusted Crab Cakes, to name a few. No matter what you choose off of this satisfying menu, it promises to please.

Additionally, Cherry Street Ease offers daily specials on top of the regular menu, including Sunday Night Turkey Dinner (starting Labor Day), Monday Night Mexican, Tuesday Night Homecookin’, Italian Night Wednesdays and Friday Night Smoked Ribs.

Happy Hour is offered on weekdays from 4 to 6 p.m. Come once to Cherry Street East, restaurant in Fairfield County, and you will see why this “hometown pub” has been a staple for over 30 years in Fairfield County, Connecticut!
